## Visualizing Worldwide Energy Production Data from 2000-2024

**Table Description**: This [Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/elvisbui/renewable-energy-share-by-country-2000-2025) dataset contains energy production/consumption in TWh, population, and GDP for 309 countries and regions from 2000-2025. As much of the 2025 data is unpopulated or incomplete, the analysis and visualization was kept to 2000-2024.
